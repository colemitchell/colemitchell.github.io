English materialists: Hobbes, Cavendish
Thu., Feb. 6
In this class, we take a brief look at a couple of English materialists who develop their views partly in reaction against Descartes’ combination of mechanical physics and mind-body dualism. Hobbes takes a fairly clear-cut reductionist approach, and Cavendish takes a more enigmatic approach that in some sense attributes life and mentality to all matter.
