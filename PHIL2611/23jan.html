Chapter 1: Epistemological Questions
Wed., Jan. 23
In this class, we discuss Chapter 1 of the Feldman textbook. §I develops “The Standard View”, which includes different kinds of commonly-accepted knowledge and sources thereof. §II presents three classic epistemological questions that can be directed at The Standard View. Finally, §III briefly considers three theories that would challenge The Standard View.
Key Concepts: The A Priori, Skepticism, Naturalized Epistemology, Relativ­ism
