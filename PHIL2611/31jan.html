Chapter 3: Modifying The Traditional Analysis of Knowledge
Thu., Jan. 31
In this class, we begin discussing Chapter 3 of the Feldman textbook. §I introduces the Gettier problem—a problem for the traditional JTB analysis of knowledge. §II explains why it won't be easy to answer this objection without modifying the analysis. §III presents two modifications that might solve the problem, and explains why they might not succeed. It finishes by proposing a mere sketch of a solution.
Key Concepts: Gettier cases, The ‘No False Grounds’ Theory, The ‘No Defeaters’ Theory
