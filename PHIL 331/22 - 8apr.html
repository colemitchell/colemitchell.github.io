Fodor on reduction
Wed., Apr. 8
This online class session covers Fodor’s critique of ‘reductivism’ about the special sciences (more commonly called reductionism), and his ‘non-reductive physicalism’.
Key Concepts: Intertheoretic Reduction, Token Physicalism, Non-Reductive Physicalism
