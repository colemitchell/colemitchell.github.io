Nagel on what it’s like
Wed., Apr. 22
This online class session covers Thomas Nagel’s critique of physicalist accounts that leave out the subjective character of experience: “what it’s like to be” a certain type of organism. He argues that it seems impossible for there to be an objective theory of consciousness, and that we do not yet understand how physicalism could be true.
Key Concepts: Objective and Subjective, Consciousness
