Horgan on superdupervenience
Wed., Apr. 15
This online class session covers Horgan’s critique of supervenience as insufficient for a satisfactory materialism. According to Horgan, more is needed than mere supervenience, and materialists face a very difficult task.
Key Concepts: Supervenience, Emergentism
Readings:
Horgan, “From Supervenience to Superdupervenience” (Textbook ch. 20)
